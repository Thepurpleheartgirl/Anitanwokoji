About Me

Hello! I'm Anita Nwokoji

I am the Purple Heart Girl — a storyteller, creative strategist, and visual artist who transforms ideas into meaningful experiences through videography, editing, voice-over, and spoken word.

My work centers on three pillars:

Poetry

Poetry informs my voice and approach to storytelling, helping me craft performances and pieces that move, inspire, and provoke thought.

Media with Purple

  • Video creation — from concept through to the final cut.
  • Voice-over — warm, clear, and professional narration for a range of projects.
  • Content creation & storytelling — strategic narratives to connect with audiences.
  • Social media management — growing and curating an authentic digital presence.

Purple Heart Girl Foundation

Giving back is central to my mission. The foundation supports mental health advocacy, youth empowerment, and community development through workshops, mentorship, and campaigns.
